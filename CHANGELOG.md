### Added

- Swift Package library product named `BurningPaper`.
- Interactive SwiftUI burning-paper surface with tap and drag ignition.
- Programmatic point, path, and reset commands.
- Configurable procedural paper, burn propagation, char, heat, flame, smoke,
  and ember rendering.
- Lower-level Metal renderer for custom integrations.
- Package-powered iOS Example application.
